OUR TEAM

Grow Life Ponics started in 2019, when four engineers from IMT Atlantique decided to do their part in developing a different agriculture, conscious of today’s environmental stakes.

The Grow Life Ponics team is made of students with experience in various fields, from advanced computer science and electronics to environment.

Grow Life Ponics specialized in popularizing domestic hydroponics, to help people grow their own food using less resources and without any pesticides.

Our goal is to make this technology as modular and as easy to understand as possible, and we developed our first product according to this vision.

OUR VALUES

At Grow Life Ponics, we are deeply committed to the values of the company :
> Support a greener way to grow crops at home, using recycled materials and proposing our own nutrients and components
> Make Hydroponic technologies accessible and easy to use, through a smartphone app
> Provide a modular system that can adapt to the room and still remain pretty and fully functionnal
